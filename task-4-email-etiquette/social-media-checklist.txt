 Do’s
- Use strong privacy settings to control who sees your posts.
- Share academic and professional achievements to build your digital presence.
- Think before posting — ensure content is respectful and appropriate.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Engage positively with peers and communities online.
 Don’ts
- Don’t post offensive, discriminatory, or harmful content.
- Don’t overshare personal details like phone numbers or addresses.
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative arguments.
- Don’t rely on social media during study/work hours excessively.
- Don’t plagiarize or copy others’ work without credit.
