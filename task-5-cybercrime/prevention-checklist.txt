Part B – Prevention Checklist 
Stay Safe Online Checklist
- Always verify the sender’s email address before clicking links.
- Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords over email or chat.
- Use strong, unique passwords and enable two‑factor authentication.
- Avoid clicking suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments.
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches.
- For UPI/online payments:
- Double‑check the recipient’s name before transferring money.
- Use official banking apps only, not third‑party shortcuts.
- Log out of accounts when using shared or public computers.
- Report suspicious activity immediately.
Reporting Channels:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
